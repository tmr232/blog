I'm signing out from Mastodon.

I love the platform, and I've had many good interactions on it.
But it no longer feels like a safe space for me.
There is only so much that content filters can do to fend off hostility.
And while that hostility is not directed at me, and the views behind it may sometimes align with my own,
it is still smothering.
We could all do with a bit more nuance and empathy.

If you want to chat, you can email me at "blog" at this website.
